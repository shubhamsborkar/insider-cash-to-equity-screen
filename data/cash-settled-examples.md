# Cash-settled / "in the form of cash" elections excluded from the insider-election run

These file as **acquisitions (code A)** but settle economically in **cash**, not stock —
a deferred paycheck indexed to the share price, economically neutral. Excluded from the
real-election list for exactly that reason. Footnote text below is **verbatim** from the raw Form 4 XML.

Scope note: my discovery set was filtered to filings containing election language, so it did
**not** contain any filing using the literal words "payable only in cash" or "phantom." The
genuine cash-settlement examples it *did* contain are below. The strongest is Enviri (NVRI),
whose security is literally titled **"Deferred Stock Unit (Cash)."**

---

## 1. Enviri Corp (NVRI) — the clean one  ⭐
- **Insider:** John S. Quinn (non-employee Director)
- **Security title (as filed):** `Deferred Stock Unit (Cash)` — code A (acquired), 3,155 units, $20.80 reference
- **Accession:** `0002104052-26-000103`
- **EDGAR:** https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1380533/000210405226000103/0002104052-26-000103-index.htm
- **Verbatim footnote [F3]:**

> "Deferred Stock Units represent deferred cash compensation awarded based on an election by the reporting person in connection with the reporting person's service as a non-employee director of the Issuer. Each Deferred Stock Unit, following vesting, represents the right to receive the value, in cash, of 1 share of the Issuer's common stock at the time of the reporting person's elected distribution date(s). The Deferred Stock Units generally vest in installments at the end of each fiscal quarter in 2026. The reporting person elected to receive settlement and distribution of the Deferred Stock Units in five equal annual installments beginning in 2027."

The phrase **"the right to receive the value, in cash, of 1 share"** is the giveaway: it never becomes stock.

## 2. Mosaic Co (MOS) — director took half in cash
- **Insider:** Kelvin R. Westbrook (Director)  [Cheryl K. Beebe filed the identical election]
- **Security:** Restricted Stock Units (settled 50% cash / 50% shares)
- **Accession:** `0001285785-26-000081`  (Beebe: `0001285785-26-000085`)
- **EDGAR:** https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1190588/000128578526000081/0001285785-26-000081-index.htm
- **Verbatim footnote [F1]:**

> "The reporting person, pursuant to the terms of the Restricted Stock Unit Agreement under which the award of restricted stock units was made, elected to receive 50% of the restricted stock units in the form of cash with the balance paid in the form of shares of common stock at the time the award was paid."

**"in the form of cash"** for half the award — the opposite of the conviction signal; the elected-cash half is economically neutral. (David T. Seaton's companion filing elected 35% in cash.)
